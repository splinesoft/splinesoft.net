MUDRammer: A Modern MUD Client

A modern MUD client for iPhone and iPad focused on speed, readability, and flexibility.

MUDs — Multi-User Dungeons — are online multiplayer text-based games. There are thousands, set in fantasy, medieval, Sci-Fi, absurdist, and many other worlds.

MUDRammer has first-class support for blind and low-vision users, recognized as an AppleVis Editorial Pick for October 2013!
