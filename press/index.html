MUDRammer - Press

A Modern MUD Client for iPhone and iPad focused on speed, readability, and flexibility.

MUDs — Multi-User Dungeons — are online multiplayer text-based games. There are thousands of them online today, set in fantasy, medieval, Sci-Fi, absurdist, and many other worlds. Some of them have been online for decades and host hundreds of concurrent players.

MUDRammer has first-class support for blind and low-vision users, recognized as an AppleVis Editorial Pick for October 2013 and earning a Golden Apple nomination!
